    CS HISTORY

    Welcome to the College of Computer Science and Information Technology of The University of Manila. We’re committed to advancing the state of the art in Computer Science and Information Technology, and to providing a challenging and exciting education for our students. Faculty members maintain active programs of research and publication in computer vision, programming languages, machine learning, databases, modeling and simulation, and the theory of computation. We offer academic programs leading to the degree of Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, and Associate in Computer Technology. Our students have the opportunity to carry out independent study and to collaborate with faculty on research projects.

    General Objectives:

    The University of Manila College of Computer Science / Information Technology aims to produce technological and scientifically empowered professionals, informational technology / computer science experts whose breadth and depth of orientation, knowledge and training, are within global standards, who shall be committed to share their expertise with the various sectors of the academic community and the community at large, ever motivated to share their concepts, their technological skills, their persons to everyone embedded in the UM trilogy of ideals: Love of Country (Patria), Love of Science (Scientia), and Love of Virtue (Virtus).

Under the vigilant stewardship of Dr. Virgilio de los Santos, the University has been placed to a higher level of credit and standard. In line with the thrusts of the government and to keep abreast with the demands of the modern times, on February 16, 1994, the University offered four-year courses in Computer Science. To show further that the University of Manila has come of age and in response to the call of the 90’s, on February 21, 1994, it offered new courses in the following programs: Doctor of Education, Doctor of Public Administration, and Master of Business Administration.

    CS EVENTS
    The Department of Computer Science hosts various events throughout the academic year. These events range from CS Acquaintanace Parties, Musical, Sports Fest, Mr. Cs and Ms. CS as well as Ms. Bebot.

    Acquaintanace Party
    The Department of Computer Science hosts Acquaintanace Parties to welcome the new freshmen and transfer students of the Computer Science course yearly as to establish bonds and to socialize the students and teachers with each other. This can be done through a talent showcase between each year through the first year freshmen up to the 4th year seniors. After the talent show, a well-deserved rest feast is done to celebrate the event and the effort of the students.
    Musical
    The Department of Computer Science hosts Musical events to celebrate and commemorate the talents of the Computer Science students as they join a competition of musical performance with each other. This event helps students to showcase their talents and skills in front of their peers as well as to build connections with each other.
    Sports Fest
    The Department of Computer Science also boasts the the talents of its students in the area of sports mainly basketball and volleyball. The department promotes physical skills and talents of each student from different year level. This is also a way of bonding moment for students with each other.
    Mr. & Ms. Cs and Ms. Bebot
    Other than sports, the department also boasts their knowledge and personal skills in the pageant Mr. & Ms. CS and Ms. Bebot. Using their intelligence and charisma, each contestant must showoff to be the Mr. & Ms. CS and Ms. Bebot.
